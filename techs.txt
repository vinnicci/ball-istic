no special, balanced overall

freeze tech:
deal freeze damage -> reduce enemy speed, suppress charge attack

electro tech:
deal electro damage -> massive shield damage, suppress shield regen

heavy tech:
no shields, massive hp capacity, slow, increased weapon overall power, explosives, mobile repair tech

covert tech:
balanced shield and hp, high mobility, long range weapons, strong charge attacks, cloaking, and disguise tech

high tech:
massive shield, low hp, phase tech

experimental tech:
niche, high risk high reward mechanics for weapons and passives
